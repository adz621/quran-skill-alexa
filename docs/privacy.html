Quran for Alexa

Privacy Policy

Last updated 12 June 2026

Quran for Alexa (“the skill”) is a free, non-commercial Alexa skill operated by Arctos Solutions. This policy explains what the skill stores, why, and the choices you have. We have tried to keep it plain and honest.

What the skill stores

To remember your settings and let you resume playback, the skill stores a small amount of data tied to the anonymous user identifier that Amazon provides for your use of the skill:

  • Your chosen reciter.
  • Your playback settings (single-chapter or continuous, and whether loop is on or off).
  • Your most recent playback position, so the skill can resume where you stopped.

That is the full extent of it. The skill does not collect or store your name, email address, postal address, contacts, location, or voice recordings. Amazon processes your speech and sends the skill only the resulting request (for example, “play chapter 1”); the skill never receives audio of your voice.

Why it is stored

The data above exists for one purpose: to make the skill work the way you would expect across sessions, so you do not have to re-select your reciter or settings each time, and so playback can resume.

Where it is stored

Settings are stored in Amazon Web Services (AWS) DynamoDB. The data is kept until you remove it (see below) or until the skill is discontinued.

What we never do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not show advertising.
  • We do not use your data to build a profile of you or track you across other services.

Third parties

  • Amazon Alexa handles the voice interaction and provides the anonymous user identifier. Your use of Alexa is governed by Amazon’s own privacy policy.
  • Amazon Web Services hosts the skill’s code and the settings store described above.
  • Quran Foundation provides the recitation audio that the skill plays. When audio plays, your Alexa device streams it from the audio provider.

Removing your data

You can have your stored settings deleted at any time by disabling Quran for Alexa in the Alexa app, or by emailing contact@arctossolutions.com and asking us to delete them. There is no other personal information to remove.

Children

The skill is suitable for listeners of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@arctossolutions.com.
