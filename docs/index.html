Quran for Alexa

Alexa skill

Recitation of the Holy Quran, on your Echo.

A free, ad-free skill that plays Quran recitation aloud. Choose your reciter, play a single chapter or listen straight through, and pick up where you left off.

Just ask

  • “Alexa, open Quran for Alexa.”Starts the skill
  • “Play chapter 1.”Plays the chapter you name
  • “Play chapter 2, verse 5.”Begins from a specific verse
  • “Change the reciter.”Reads out the list so you can choose by number
  • “Turn on loop.”Repeats when playback reaches the end
  • “Pause” · “Resume” · “Next.”Standard playback controls

What it does

  • Choose your reciterSelect from a curated list by number.
  • Single or continuousStop at the end of a chapter, or keep playing through the next.
  • LoopRepeat a chapter, or the whole Quran, when it finishes.
  • Remembers youYour reciter and playback settings are kept for next time.

Free and non-commercial. Quran for Alexa is offered without charge and without advertising. Recitation audio is provided by the Quran Foundation. This skill is a player only and is not affiliated with Amazon.
